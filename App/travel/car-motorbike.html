Preperation

If you plan on driving to work, the barriers are operated automatically by a vehicle number plate recognition service. In order to avoid causing any delays to regular traffic while getting onto the site, it is essential that you provide us with the following details of your vehicle in advance:

  • Registration number
  • Make
  • Model
  • Colour
Register your vehicle

Please email the above details to accommodation@ipo.gov.uk as a matter of priority. If you do not provide these details, you will be stopped by security on entering the site. Providing that you confirm these details with them, they will be able to direct you where to appropriately park.

Parking facilities

Occasionally, they will provide you with a reserved parking bay which is outside the office's entrance. Alternatively, there is a multi-storey car park located on-site which they may direct you to. Once your car details are logged with the accommodation department, you shouldn't be stopped at the security desk and you can drive straight on-site.

Motorbike

You should register your motorbike the same way you would a car. The multi-storey car park has speciality parking spaces reserved for motorbikes on the ground floor.
