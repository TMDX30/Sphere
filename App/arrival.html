Arrival
Entering our site

If you are travelling by car and your car registration is not yet on the system you will need to pull into the lay by when your registration flags up. A security guard will ask for you details and once they know you are a new starter they will direct you to our multi-storey car park.

If you walk in through the main gate there is a turnstile (that requires a security badge) and a separate door that is usually closed. There is a button you can press to get the security guards attention in the box and speak to them. You can press this button, explain it is your first day and the guard will allow you to pass through the turnstile.
