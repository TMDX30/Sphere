Introduction
Good luck!

Congratulations on taking up your new post at the Intellectual Property Office (IPO). By now you will have read your new starter pack which should have provided you with basic information about your role. This app is designed to provide you with information you might find helpful before joining the office and in your first few days.

The office

The Intellectual Property Office has about 1100 employees, split across 3 sites:

  • Newport – Concept house
  • London – Abbey orchard
  • Nine Mile point
Departments

Within this there is a total of 8 directorates that make up the IPO:

  • Copyright and Enforcement Directorate
  • Finance
  • Innovation Directorate
  • International Policy Directorate
  • Information Technology Directorate
  • People, Places and Services
  • Patents Directorate
  • Tribunals, Trade Marks and Design Directorate
