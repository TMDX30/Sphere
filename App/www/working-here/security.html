Introduction

Our aim is to deliver a safe and secure environment (be it online or in the office) where individuals, business and government, can enjoy the full benefits of IPO services.

This should aid in the facilitation of collaborative working in the Office, at home and even overseas. This will enable us to promote and maximise UK Intellectual Property and our systems and procedures.

When you arrive at the office you will need to present your staff pass to the security pass at either the staff entrance or general reception.

Don’t panic if you get to the office and suddenly realise you’ve forgotten your pass! You will be able to get a temporary pass from the security guard for the day once they have verified your identity.
