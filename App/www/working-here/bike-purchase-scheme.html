Bike Purchase Scheme
Introduction

‘Cycle to work’ is designed to promote healthier journeys to work while reducing environmental pollution. It allows employers to loan cycles and cycle safety equipment to staff as a tax-free benefit.

You can choose to purchase a bicycle through the “IPO: On Your Bike” scheme. This consists of a small contribution taken from your pay each month for the following 12 months. This scheme and is open to all staff.

Cost

You can sacrifice up to £1000 of your annual wage over the 12 month period providing this doesn’t take your pay lower than the national minimum wage. For staff that this affects, an extension of the 12 months will be considered.

What about leisure?

You can definitely use it for leisure purposes! Guidelines say that 50% of the bike use is to be used for getting to and from work.
