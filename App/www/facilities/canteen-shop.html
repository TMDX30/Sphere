Canteen & Shop
Introduction

There is a shared canteen located within the ONS. It is open throughout the day, with breakfast served from 8-11am and lunch served from 11:30am-2:15pm with a varied selection of both hot and cold meals available.

Shop

Next door to the canteen is a small shop which is open all day from 8am - 5pm. Here, you can purchase daily necessities whether it's a newspaper, crisps, chocolate, sandwiches or even Paracetamol.
