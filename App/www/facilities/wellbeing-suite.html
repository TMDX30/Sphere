Wellbeing Suite
Introduction

Unveiled at the start of 2016, the Wellbeing Suite offers a variety of activities to allow staff to unwind whilst on their own time. These include:

  • Pool table
  • Snooker table
  • Dart board
  • Board games
  • Video game consoles

Whilst you will be free to make use of the services of the Wellbeing suite you will have to do so on your own time. This means that should you wish to play a game of Pool or Snooker (or use any of the other facilities in the Wellbeing Suite) you must clock-out using the Flexi terminals located around the building.
