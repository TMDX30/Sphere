Parking
Introduction

Parking is always a contentious issue in any work place though we aim to make this experience as pleasant as possible. Concept house has its own on site, secured, multi-storey car park as well as a number of uncovered bays.

An on-site parking space is not a guarantee though, on peak working days (which tend to be Tuesday to Thursdays) there may not been any free spaces left after a certain time, on these occasions you are able to use the car park in Tredegar Park which is reasonably close to the office.

However you should be aware that the IPO takes no responsibility for cars and belongings parked in Tredegar Park. If you are part of the car-share scheme you may be eligible for a reserved bay.
