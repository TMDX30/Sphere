Introduction

Once fully settled into your role at the IPO, you will be given a desk with a storage pedestal. You can lock them and store personal belongings securely.

The majority of desks use small remote PC's that enable you to connect to your desktop from anywhere in the office. In some roles, you may be allocated a physical machine.

All desks have a a telephone. You can use this to contact your collegues accross the organisation.
