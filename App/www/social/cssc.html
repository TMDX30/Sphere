Introduction

By becoming a Civil Service Sports Council (CSSC) member you will receive a number of benefits including a Countdown savings card which entitles you to receive discount at most high street stores.

You will receive a National Savings Guide which contains money off deals and coupons for holidays, days out, restaurants, insurance and much more. It also entitles you to play in many sporting events locally and nationally, as well as participating in the BIS sports day.
