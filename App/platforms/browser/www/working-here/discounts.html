Introduction

The IPO discounts scheme is available to all IPO staff. Employees will be able to buy from a wide range of high street vendors, department stores and retails brands. This is provided through an online portal by Xexec which is accessible from work or at home.

The scheme is free to all IPO staff. Discounts include everything from theatre tickets through to holidays, gifts, shopping and home services. You will need to register using your staff number (SE number) which can be found on EtarmisÂ® or on your payslip.
