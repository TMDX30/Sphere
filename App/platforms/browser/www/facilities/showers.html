Showers
Introduction

As well as showering facilities available in the gym changing rooms, there are further showers available for those who cycle to and from the office. These are in self-contained cubicles. If you have difficulty finding them, just ask another member of staff who will assist you.

Adjacent to the shower facilities, there are a limited number of lockers available which you can book via Accommodation. There is a long waiting list to be allocated a locker so it is good advice to make this request as early as possible if you wish to use the locker facilities.
