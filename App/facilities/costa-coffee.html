Introduction

The office has a Costa Coffee shop, located in the neighbouring ONS building. Costa provides a good opportunity to get out of the regular office environment. The shop has a small library of books and can be used to relax or even as an environment for informal meetings. There are occasional market and craft stalls that sell bespoke gifts and items.
