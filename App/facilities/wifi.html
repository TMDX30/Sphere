Wi-fi
Introduction

The office has complimentary Wi-Fi available to all staff that can be accessed with a username and password via a one-time registration. You will receive notification of your personal username and password once you arrive at the office. Once you have registered the first time, you should be able to log-in without having to input any further details.

Wi-fi is also offered to visitors on site and this can be accessed with a username and password from reception.

There is an acceptable use policy for wi-fi which you can find on Circle when you arrive in the office. It is strongly advised that you follow this policy when accessing the internet on your personal device
