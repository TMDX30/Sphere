Wellbeing Suite
Introduction

Unveiled at the start of 2016, the Wellbeing Suite offers a variety of activities to allow staff to unwind whilst at work. These include:

  • Pool Table
  • Snooker Table
  • Dart Board
  • Board Games
  • Video Games

Staff are reminded that time spent in the Wellbeing Suite does not count towards their working hours.
