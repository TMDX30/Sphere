Discounts
Introduction

The IPO discounts scheme is available to all IPO staff. Employees will be able to buy from a wide range of high street vendors, department stores and retails brands. Through an online portal supplied by Xexec which is accessible from work or at home.

The scheme is free to all IPO staff.

Discounts include everything from tickets for theatres through to holidays, gifts, shopping and home services.

You will need to register using your staff number (SE number), this can be found on your payslip.
