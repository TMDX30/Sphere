Nursery

The office offers nursery facilities at Acorns Nursery, part of the Office of National Statistics, our neighbouring office.

This purpose built nursery cares for 33 children each day, with babies being cared for in their own suite of rooms. The nursery is equipped to the highest standards and our spacious gardens provide areas for children to play, rest and learn about the great outdoors.
