Introduction

Benenden Healthcare is a 'not-for-profit' friendly society and provides an affordable alternative to private medical insurance for employees of the Civil Service. This service offers a reduction to waiting times for consultations and treatments and covers all benefits in one price with no exclusions for pre-existing conditions.
