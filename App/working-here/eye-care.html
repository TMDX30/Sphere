Eye Care
Introduction

The Office is committed to maintaining a safe working environment for everyone and in accordance with Health and Safety (Display Screen Equipment) Regulations 1992 is required to provide appropriate eye tests for staff working with Display Screen Equipment (DSE).

If you work with computers and wish to have an eye test, contact our HR advisors at hradvisors@ipo.gov.uk. HR will then give you a form for yourself and your optician to fill out. Once you have handed the form back to HR you will be reimbursed for the cost of the eye test up to a limit of £20.

The office will meet the cost of DSE corrective glasses up to a limit of £60 if prescribed by the optician.
