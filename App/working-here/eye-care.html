Eye Care
Introduction

Staff who use a computer display as part of their job can obtain a form from HR to take to their optician. This form will cover your visit to the optician up to a limit of £20. If as a result of the eye test the optician advises that normal glasses or lenses cannot be used for computer display work and that special corrective glasses are required, the IPO will meet the cost of the corrective glasses up to a limit of £60.
