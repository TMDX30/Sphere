Introduction

Congratulations on taking up your new post at the Intellectual Property Office (IPO). This app is designed to provide you with all the information you might find helpful before joining the office.

Security gate

If you are travelling by car or walking it is likely you will be stopped by our security desk before entering the site who will ask you to confirm your details. Once you have done this, they will direct you to main reception. The main reception is signposted on-site should you forget what the security guards tell you.
